Our Mission

Ensley Orthodontics is a welcoming place with service above and beyond the expectations of all whom we encounter. It is our goal to provide excellent care while educating and meeting each individual’s needs with respect and integrity.

Our philosophy at Ensley Orthodontics is centered on you. It can be summed up in two words: patient advocacy. We are guided by a set of principles we call P3: Patients First, Passion, and the Pursuit of Excellence. Our focus on these principles reminds us what is most important in delivering on our mission.

Our Approach

When you’re our patient, you’re more than just an x-ray—you’re an important individual, and we treat you that way. We focus on communicating clearly with you and with each other, emphasizing teamwork so that your orthodontic care is personalized, efficient, and provides a positive experience you will value for a lifetime.

Clinical excellence really is difficult to find. But for us, it’s non-negotiable. That’s why we keep up with the latest orthodontic technology and aim to deliver care that’s beyond your expectations. No longer a painful series of events, orthodontic treatment can be a positive experience from which you can grow.

As a patient, it won’t take you long to notice our love for our work. It’s no struggle to foster a fun work environment here. In addition to being passionate about and loving the orthodontics field, we truly enjoy each other’s company. Come visit and see for yourself how different your orthodontic experience can be with Ensley Orthodontics!
